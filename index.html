6 Reagent Slots. One Box.

Most systems require an equipment cabinet and a tangle of tubing. Wavie packs six complete reagent bays into a footprint no competitor can match. Test alkalinity, calcium, magnesium, nitrate, phosphate, and more — automatically, on a schedule you control.

Precision Engineering. Every Detail.

Built by reef hobbyists who know what matters. Proprietary valve and pump architecture engineered for long-term reliability — not an off-the-shelf lab instrument dressed up for aquariums. Every component chosen with your sump in mind.

Smaller Than Anything Else.

Neptune's systems are bulky by design. Wavie was built compact-first — it tucks into a sump cabinet, sits on a shelf, or mounts to a wall. No compromises on capability. Just less space consumed and a cleaner setup.
