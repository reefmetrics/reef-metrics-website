Week 1 Hardware Mechanical Latest

Kickoff: The State of the Hardware

March 26, 2026  •  5 min read

This is where we're starting. The core mechanical assembly is complete — the chassis, reagent bay layout, and valve mounting are locked in. This week we're documenting the current state before we begin the iteration push toward beta.

The six-slot reagent design has been validated for fit and flow. Each slot accepts a standard reagent vessel and routes through the proprietary pinch valve mechanism we designed in-house. The footprint is significantly smaller than comparable systems — this was a non-negotiable design constraint from day one.

What still needs work: the pump mounting needs a revision to reduce vibration transfer to the chassis, and we're finalizing the optical path geometry for the spectrometer module. Both are on the schedule for the next two weeks.
